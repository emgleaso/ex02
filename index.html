Heyawake 

     Color the cells of the
grid either black or white
according to the rules:
  • Regions with a number should contain black cells matching the number
  • Black cells cannot be adjacent horizontally or vertically
  • A straight line of connected white cells cannot span across more than 2 regions
  • All white cells must be connected in a single group

Nurikabe 

     Color the cells of the
grid either black or white
according to the rules:
  • The black cells form the stream, which must be orthogonally contiguous, number-free, and containing no 2x2 or larger solid rectangles
  • The white cells form islands, where each number n must be part of an n-omino composed only of white cells
  • Each island must have exactly one numbered cell, and a white cell can belong to only one island

Akari 

     Place light bulbs in
the grid according to the rules:
  • A cell is illuminated by a light bulb if they are in the same row or column, and if there are no black cells between them
  • No light bulb may illuminate another light bulb
  • Some black cells contain numbers indicating how many lightbulbs are orthogonally touching that cell
